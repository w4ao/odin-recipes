Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh or store-bought pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup of homemade or store-bought Pizza Sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded low-moisture Mozzarella
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly grated Parmesean
  • 1 pack of sliced Pepperoni
  • Cornmeal for the pan

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F.
  2. Sprinkle a dusting of cornmeal onto the pizza pan.
  3. Stretch the dough out into a 12-inch circle onto the pizza pan.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough.
  5. Add your mozzarella over the pizza evenly.
  6. Add desired amount of Pepperoni over the pizza.
  7. Spinkle the freshly grated Parmesean over the pizza.
  8. Bake for 8-12 minuted or until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Slice the pizza and serve.
