Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of grape jelly
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Directions

  1. Take one slice of bread and spread the peanut butter evenly on one side.
  2. Take the other slice of bread and spread the jelly across evenly on one side.
  3. Put both slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly in the middle.
  4. Preheat a pan on low to medium heat.
  5. Spread the butter evenly on both sides of the sandwich.
  6. Place the sandwich in the pan and toast on both sides until golden brown.
  7. Slice and serve.
